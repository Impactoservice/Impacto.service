OUR POLICY

Payment & Progress Policy

Our client-friendly approach ensures you only pay when you see results.

No Progress, No Payment

At Impacto Service, we believe in results-driven work. Our policy ensures that you only pay when you see movement:

  • Payment is only required after measurable progress is demonstrated
  • We provide regular progress reports with clear metrics
  • Clients approve each phase before any payment is processed
  • Transparent tracking of all key performance indicators

Payment Structure

Our flexible payment options are designed to align with your business growth:

  • Milestone-based payments: Pay as we achieve agreed-upon objectives
  • Performance-based pricing: Fees tied to specific measurable outcomes
  • Monthly retainers: For ongoing services with progress checkpoints
  • Custom payment plans: Tailored to your cash flow and business cycle

Progress Measurement

We define and track progress using clear, objective metrics:

  • Pre-defined KPIs established during onboarding
  • Regular reporting intervals (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly)
  • Dashboard access to real-time performance data
  • Quarterly business reviews to assess overall impact

Client Protection

Your satisfaction and financial security are our top priorities:

  • 30-day money-back guarantee if progress standards aren't met
  • Contract flexibility to adjust scope based on results
  • No long-term lock-in contracts
  • Direct access to account managers for any concerns

Getting Started

Our onboarding process ensures alignment from day one:

  • Free initial consultation to assess your needs
  • Custom proposal with defined success metrics
  • Progress measurement framework development
  • Flexible start dates with no upfront payment required
